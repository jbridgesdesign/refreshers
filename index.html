We Love Refreshers

Refreshers are the best! They are so delicious and refreshing, especially on a hot day. We love all the different flavors, but our favorites are strawberry and watermelon. Refreshers are the perfect way to cool down and quench your thirst. We can't get enough of them!

Refreshers Release Timeline

  1. 2005: The first refresher flavor, Strawberry, is released.
  2. 2007: Watermelon and Lemon flavors are introduced.
  3. 2010: Grape flavor is added to the lineup.
  4. 2015: Blue Raspberry flavor is launched, becoming an instant hit.
  5. 2020: New limited-edition flavors are released each summer, keeping fans excited and refreshed!
