We Love Refreshers

Refreshers Release Timeline

  • 2012: Starbucks Refreshers are introduced as low-calorie, canned energy drinks and Via Instant packets. (1) Starbucks introduces all-new hand-crafted Refreshers - Cool Lime and Very Berry Hibiscus. (2)
  • 2013: Starbucks later introduces new Valencia Orange hand-craft Refresher. (3)
  • 2015: Starbucks then introduces the fan-favorite Strawberry Açaí Refresher, hitting it off the charts with the introduction of the Pink Drink - a mix of the Refresher base and coconut milk. (4)
  • 2018: The popular Mango Dragonfruit Refresher is introduced. (5)
  • 2020: The Kiwi Starfruit Refresher is introduced. (6)
  • 2024: The Summer Berry Refresher is introduced as a seasonal flavor, including boba-like Raspberry Pearls. (7)
Last updated: February 25, 2026
